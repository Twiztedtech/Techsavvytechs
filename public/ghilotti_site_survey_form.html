Step 1 of 7

General Project Info & Objectives

Critical Engineering Guideline

Transition Note: The G41 is a traditional RF-over-Coax booster splitting signal dynamically over thick LMR400. The new Cel-Fi QUATRA EVO functions differently. The Network Unit (NU) digitizes the signal and transmits it to remote Coverage Units (CUs) over Category Cables (Cat5e/Cat6). You must identify dedicated network cable paths between buildings. Coax cannot carry the signal between NU and CUs.
